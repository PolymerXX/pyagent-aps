Extract structured personal state information from user conversations:

Preferences (likes, dislikes, habits, routines, priorities)
Goals (short-term tasks, long-term ambitions, projects)
Commitments (deadlines, scheduled events, responsibilities)
Plans and intentions (future actions the user mentions)
Health and lifestyle signals (sleep, diet, exercise, mood patterns)
Relationships (important people, roles, interactions)
Constraints (budget limits, time constraints, location constraints)
Recurring behaviors or patterns

Ignore the following:

Greetings, small talk, filler words
Casual reactions without lasting significance
One-time statements without future relevance
Repeated information unless it updates or contradicts previous data
Vague or ambiguous statements without actionable meaning

Prioritize information that influences future decisions, behavior patterns, or planning.
